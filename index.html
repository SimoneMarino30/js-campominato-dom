Welcome to
a challenging and exciting game of strategy and luck! In MineDOM, your mission is to clear a grid filled with hidden mines while avoiding them. Let's get started!

Objective:

  • Your goal is to uncover all the safe cells on the grid without clicking on any of the hidden mines.
  • The number of mines remains constant at 16, but the grid size varies based on the difficulty level.

Difficulty Levels:

Minefield offers three different difficulty levels, each with a unique grid size:

  1. Easy: A beginner-friendly option with a 10x10 grid (100 cells) and 16 mines.
  2. Medium: A moderate challenge with a 9x9 grid (81 cells) and 16 mines.
  3. Hard: The ultimate test of skill, featuring a 7x7 grid (49 cells) with 16 mines.

Gameplay:

  1. Choose your preferred difficulty level before starting the game.
  2. Click on a cell to reveal its content. If it's a mine, you lose the game.
  3. Continue clicking on cells until you've uncovered all safe cells or accidentally clicked on a mine.

Winning:

You win the game by successfully uncovering all the safe cells without clicking on any mines.

Losing:

If you click on a mine, you lose the game. But don't worry; you can always try again!

Tips:

  • Use logic and deduction to make educated guesses about safe cells.
  • Plan your moves carefully, especially in the harder difficulty levels.

Are you ready to test your skills and conquer the MineDOM? Select your preferred difficulty level and start playing. Good luck, and may you navigate the mineDOM safely!
